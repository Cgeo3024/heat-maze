Solo Room

Choose one of the rooms below to get started.

Each one provides a target temperature you need certain positions to meet. Be careful not to exceed any limits though!

Each room is slightly different.

Room A features a single 15cm insulated copper bar.
There is a heat source at one end, and your goal is to get the temperature of the other end of the bar up to 30 Degrees.

Room B features a single 10cm insulated copper bar.
There is a heat source at one end, and your goal is to get the temperature of the other end of the bar up to 25 Degrees.
However, no point on the bar must be above 60 Degrees at any time.

Room C features two bars. A is a 10cm iron bar, and bar B is 5cm of Tin. The end of bar A is connected to the middle of bar B
There is a heat source at one end of A, and your goal is to get the temperature of ends of Bar B up to 30 Degrees.

Room D features 3 separate bars.
Bar A is a 10cm copper bar. At one end it has a fixed 40 degree source, the other end has a goal temperature of 10 Degrees.
Bar B is a 6cm Iron bar. One end of B is a variable heat source.
Bar C is a 3cm Copper bar which connects bars A and B. It joins bar A 5cm along the bar, and joins bar B 2cm along.
